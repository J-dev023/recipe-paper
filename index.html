Scallops with vegetables

Sweet tender scallops combine with crisp crunchy vegetables in this colorful dish. If you like, you can use shrimp, clams, or mussels instead of the scallops

ingredients

  • 6-8 sea scallops
  • 2 cups vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp cornstarch mixed with 2 tsps water 2 tsps water
  • 2 cups vegeable oil
  1. 1 small carrot, thinly sliced
  2. 1/2 cup loosely packed baby corn cobs, halved lengthwise
  3. 1/3 cup canned straw mushrooms, halved lengthwise ( see box,right)
